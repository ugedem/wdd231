Site Information

Site Name:

Mkpouto Cuisine

"Mkpouto" meaning "Precious" in the Ibibio language emphasizes the value and richness of the Calabar dishes being showcased on the site.

Site Purpose:

The website serves as a digital hub for Calabar cuisine, offering traditional recipes, stories about the dishes, and restaurant recommendations. It aims to connect people with the flavors and cultural heritage of Nigeria’s Calabar region.

Target Audience Scenarios:

  1. How do I cook Afang soup and what are its cultural roots?
  2. Where can I find the best Calabar cuisine in Ibadan?
  3. What ingredients are used to make Edikang Ikong soup?

Color Schema:

The following colors were selected for the website:

  • Primary Color: Green (#2E8B57) - Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: Cream (#FFFDD0) - Used as the background color to give the site a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Typography:

The following fonts were selected for the website:

  • Primary Font: 'Roboto' (sans-serif) - Used for body text for readability and clarity.
  • Secondary Font: 'Playfair Display' (serif) - Used for headings to give the site a distinct and elegant feel.
  • Special Font: 'Dancing Script' - Used for special sections like the logo or call-to-action text to give personality and flair.
